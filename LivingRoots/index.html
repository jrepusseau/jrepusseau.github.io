F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Please feel free to contact us by discord or email.

  • I'm a farmer, how can I use your app?

    Please reach our Marketplace place, you will be able to manage your products and transfer cryptocurrency.

  • What is a wallet? How can I get one?

    A cryptocurrency wallet is the place where you will stock your crytocurrencies and digital assets. We don't know about your wallet keys! So please make sure to write your wallet seed phrase carefully.

  • I'm a NGO / Third party certification where do I start?

    We developp the Webconsole app that allow you to create certification, send them and send fund directly to farmers wallet.

  • I'm a food / beverage brand, what services can you offer?

    We can help you implement our secure, free tool that will analyze the blockchain token among the supply chain and help your consumer get certified information.

  • Can I join the community?

    Yes please! Food security is the concern of everyone and we are developing tools to put the food security at the services of consumers, farmers and regulators.
