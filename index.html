Flipview

BETA

Flipview is an open-source platform where every user can express their opinion about politics and society without censorship. The subject is usually a controversial subject and every subject is divided into several arguments. In every argument users can leave constructed comments which other users can rate according to structure, neutrality and trustability. Users can also make their sources directly accessible through the browser. Finally, user can rate their own confidence in an argument using a bayesian grade.

BodyPrograms

BodyPrograms is an AI Based cross-platform developed in 4 functions. The first one is to make a virtual model of the users included skeleton and skins. The second one will detect surface problem skin problems ( aging,infection, nevus, pimples), musculation, scarf, bad-posture ... using AI body2pix and posenet on the model. On the third step, once problem detected and labelled the responsive website offer recommendations into food change programs and sport change programs or medications-drugs use programs. The third step is the following up of the programs. For food program, pictures of the food and image detection will allow to recognize the everyday food. For sport programs the app will helps to realize movements correctly and track the users running. Back the first step the app will analyze the efficiency of the programs (foods,sport,drugs) or others change directly on the user new model.

Communitea

BETA

Communitea wants to improve nudges in order to reduce plastic waste. The main idea is to reward people for bringing their own containers and bags when buying items such as coffee, groceries, or pizza. Everytime stores notice customers bringing their own containers or bags, they will give them Communitea stickers with their own logo. On the stickers will be qr codes which will scan and accumulate reward points. Once a certain amount of points is reached, Communitea in partnership with the store will offer discount items or free items. All the stickers are unique according to the store or restaurant and when the sticker is scanned you get points, after a certain amounts of points you will have the ability to level up in the Communitea. We put the store on the Communitea map as à form of exposure advertisement amongst users. The number of people received stickers at that specific shop will be displayed on the map. Also, limited edition stickers can be designed and made in collaboration with influential artists for marketing purposes.

ARC

BETA

ARC or Autonomous Robotic City is a location-based services website designed to help the communication between people and emergency services. The original idea was to connect an interactive map with a social network to allow users to follow ambulances and fire trucks and to chat directly with them. The original version got extended to offer the possibility to the user to create events with friends, order groceries, and ask for other public services.
