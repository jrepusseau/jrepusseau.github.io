Biological aging

Lifespan or longevity refers to the duration an organism life whereas healthspan refers to the duration of good health. Since the previous lifespan and healthspan doesn’t have the same objective, well-being as a trade of these two criteria’s. Then aging biomarker is a marker that predicts a temporally later feature of well-being better than the chronological age.

The biological aging is the result of recursive, multiple, separate, diverse, interconnected, but malleable biomarkers of aging that can eventually compromising normal functions, at different times and levels. This process only occurs in somatic cells species and has been associated with fragility and multiple diseases, disabilities and morbidities.

Longevity research and treatments

Many pathways have been studied to repair biological aging

Clearance of senescent cell (Senotherapy) Stem cell addition Inflammation reduction, blood rejuvenation
Restoring mitochondrial functions Caloric restriction Reducing cell with genomic damage (Gene therapy)
Reactive protein regulation Epigenetic drugs Telomere reactivation (Telomerase)

Among those pathways, clearance of senesecent cells (senotharapy) in particular senolytics treatments is currently one of the most affodable, practical and promising research field .
