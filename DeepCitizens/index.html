DeepCitizens


Inspired by the BIM (Building Information Modeling), the CIM will be the next breakthrough for the construction industry. This technology will included building, roads, public spaces and all the public equipement.
But this technology is far to be out of resources and already several layers of information can be build on top of those model. Features about the active population, the age , the political vote, the weather, public camera, the recycling equipement...
All the imaginable data layers can be add to the model to make the perfect data alias.

The second step of this project will be the predictions or simulation of the near future of the city. What if we can optimize the placement of a next hospital, college or swimming pool? What if we can reduce the unemployement of a town or even a city? Will we predict the next election based on the results before?

The CIM Open University is a non-profit website, all data are open-sourced we will try to collaborate actively with the world. Every database-source maybe potentially flags in case of suspicion of reliability.
